## Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

- Ek Highest-Ranking Manager, Woh company ki sare vision, mission, long term goals, organization in sb chizo ke liya strategy banate hai.

### Core Responsibilities:

1. Strategic Leadership: Unka kaam hota hai vision, mission, and strategic roadmaps banana for company.
2. Big-Picture Vision: Company ka long-term goal set krte hai or determines krte hai ki kese woh company ko ek sustainable growth dila skte hai.
3. Team Leadership; Hires, guides, or baki ke top executives ki dekhna ki woh kaam sahi se krte hai ya nhi jese ki  (CFO, COO).
4. Public Faces; Woh serves krte media, investors, and public se ek primary spokesperson ki tarah.
5. Company Culture: Company ki working values, working style, and team ka overall environment and vibes.

> For Example:
> 
> 1. CEO of Amazon is “Andy Jassy”
> 
> 2. CEO of Flipkart is “Kalyan Krishnamurthy”

## Chief Technical Officer (CTO)

- Ye company ke Highest Ranking Tech executive hote hai or inka kaam hota hai ki technical area ko kese sambhalna hai.

### Core Responsibilities:

1. Big Picture Vision: Decide krna ki company ko competitors se aage rekhne ke liye konsi new technologies use krni chaiye.
2. Team Leaderships: Engineering, software, development and IT(Information Technology) ko manage krna.
3. Product Building: Apps, Websites, and software ko smooth banana taki customers easily use krle.
4. Security and Reliability: Ensure krna ki company ka tech system safe ho or hack se fast ho or crash naa ho.

> For Example:CTO of Pinterest is “Matt Madrigal”
> CTO of OpenAI is “Shrinivas Narayanan”

## Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

- Ye company ke Top Financial Executive hota hai, or CEO ka right hand bhi hote h as a advisor, inka kaam hota hai pura financial area sambhalna.

### Core Responsibilities:

1. Financial Planning: Future income predict karna & company growth ke liye budget banana.
2. Risk Management: Fraud, market crash & bad investments se company ko protect karna.
3. Reporting & Compliance: Financial records accurate rakhna & legal rules follow karna.
4. Cash Flow Management: Ensure karna ki salaries, bills & taxes ke liye hamesha enough cash ho.

> CFO of Lenskart is “Abhishek Gupta”
